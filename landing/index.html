FAQ

Questions worth asking.

How is this different from just using my brokerage app?
Brokerage apps show you raw price changes. Alphenzi shows you performance relative to the market — alpha — and quantifies exactly how much your own decisions added or subtracted value vs simply buying SPY. It also ties news events to your specific holdings and gives you AI-powered context, not just charts.
Do I need to connect my brokerage account?
No. Alphenzi never connects to your brokerage. You upload your portfolio as an Excel/CSV file or enter holdings manually. Your account numbers, credentials, and real-time balances are never seen by Alphenzi.
How does the AI work? Is my data sent to OpenAI?
AI features use a provider chain (Gemini ? OpenAI ? deterministic fallback). Portfolio context is sent only to your configured provider and only when AI features are enabled. You can disable AI at any time and keep deterministic analytics active.
I only have Canadian stocks and ETFs. Does this work for me?
Alphenzi was built with CAD-first investors in mind. It supports any TSX-listed ticker (including .TO and .V suffixes), handles real-time CAD/USD conversion, and shows values in your preferred currency. It's one of the few tools that properly handles cross-border Canadian portfolios.
Where can Alphenzi be deployed?
Alphenzi supports managed hosting and private deployment. Deployment model, support level, and data residency requirements are handled through commercial plans.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings — you keep access until the end of the billing period. Your data can be exported as CSV at any time before or after cancellation.
